MAGICSPELLS

Welcome to MAGIC SPELLS game!

This game aims to develop basic skills in areas such as grammar / listening, mathematics, geography and biology. Enjoy!

PLAY

Several rules to follow:

You are given the role of a light elf who fights a monster. Each of you has 100 health points. Each spell is a task in some field of knowledge that you have to do. In case of failure, the control is passed to the evil monster, which has a reserve of spells. But don't worry! There are spells that can cure you, thus increasing your hp. As soon as the monster is defeated, you move to the next round. Good luck!

The key use (if nessesary)
# Key Action
1 Esc Quitting the game and trigging the score
2 Tab Focusing on a special task button
3 Enter Toggling the modal dialog with task buttons and clicking on the chosen task button
4 F7 Listening the audio task

The server-side of the app was built on the heroku + mLab base
